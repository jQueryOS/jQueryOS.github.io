Roadmap

  • Bootloader

    The bootloader is the essential part of any operating system. Without it the operating system wouldn't even be able to boot

    Status: currently a barebones bootloader (barebones/) is in production. A partial bootloader on the master/ branch is in progress.

  • Kernel / IPC

    IPC or Inter-Process Communication is the mechanisms which allows processes, hosted by the OS, to communicate with either the kernel, or seperate process themselves.

    Status: IPC development will begin after various other mechanisms have been developed
